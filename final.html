Mobile Legend

SABER

Deep in the northern mountains of the Cadia Riverlands lies a quiet and peaceful village. This village is home to the legendary Tianyin Swordmaster Sect, and is also the place where Duan Meng spent his childhood perfecting his technique. As soon as he was old enough to pick up a bamboo sword, Duan Meng was determined to become a great swordmaster.

ALUCARD

Alucard is a very strong hero, when he is sufficiently farmed, especially when he is ahead in farm. He is able to burst down the squishy enemy heroes within mere seconds and yet still have the ability to continue fighting in fights with the low cooldown of his skills, bringing the team to victory if played right.

URANUS

The Celestial Palace - Legend has it that the city floating above the skies of the Land of Dawn was once home to the Gods. It is also rumored that this mysterious place holds the key to ruling the world. Eons ago, the ruler of the celestial palace utilized the technological secret of the ancients to create Uranus, then commanded it to guard the city and await the emergence of a new ruler.
